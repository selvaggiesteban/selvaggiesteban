<h2 id="que-es-marketing-digital">What is digital marketing and why is it crucial today?</h2>
<p><strong>Digital marketing</strong> encompasses all marketing strategies and actions executed through digital media and channels. <p>The relevance of digital marketing today is undeniable. <p>For businesses in Lanús, this means they can compete more effectively, even with limited budgets, if they know how to direct their messages to the right people at the right time. It's a democratizing tool that levels the playing field and rewards creativity and strategy.</p>

<h3 id="seo-local">Local SEO: Stand out in Lanús searches</h3>
<p><strong>Local SEO</strong> is one of the most powerful tools for businesses with a physical location. It consists of optimizing your online presence so your company appears in top results when a user searches for products or services "near me" or in a specific location like "restaurant in Lanús."</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Google My Business (GMB):</strong> It's your online business card. Optimize your profile with accurate information (address, phone, hours), quality photos, and actively manage your customer reviews.</li>
<li><strong>Local Keywords:</strong> Include the name of your locality (Lanús, Remedios de Escalada, Gerli) in your website content, product/service descriptions, and posts.</li>
<li><strong>Online Reviews:</strong> Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews on GMB, Facebook, and other relevant platforms. Respond to all reviews, both positive and negative, in a professional manner.</li>
<li><strong>NAP Consistency:</strong> Ensure your business Name, Address, and Phone (NAP) are identical across all online platforms (website, directories, social media).</li>
</ul>

<li><strong>Videos:</strong> Product demonstrations, virtual tours of your store, interviews with satisfied local customers, local events.</li>

</ul>
<p>Well-crafted content not only improves your SEO but also positions your business as an authority in your field and builds trust with Lanús consumers.</p>

<p itemprop="text">Investment in digital marketing varies enormously depending on the size of your business, your goals, and the competition.
