<h2>What exactly is RPA automation in web design?</h2>



<p>If you've ever wondered how you can make certain boring, routine, and highly repetitive tasks disappear from your work routine, here's the definitive answer. RPA automation consists of using software robots to perform digital tasks exactly as a flesh-and-blood person would on your page.</p>



<p>Imagine someone fills out a contact form on your site. Instead of you having to manually copy that data, send it to an Excel spreadsheet, and send a welcome email, RPA automation does absolutely all of that in seconds, without you lifting a finger in front of the monitor.</p>
