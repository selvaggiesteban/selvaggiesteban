<h3>1) What OCA service is used for WooCommerce stores?</h3>



<p>For WooCommerce (and for the <strong>Wanderlust OCA</strong> plugin), the service that OCA offers is:</p>



<blockquote class="is-layout-flow">
<p>🟣 <strong>OCA e-Pak / OCA Express Pak (service for ecommerce)</strong></p>
</blockquote>



<p>It is OCA's platform designed specifically for online stores: it allows you to manage ecommerce shipments and is the one that exposes the <strong>OCA e-Pak API</strong> that the Wanderlust plugin uses to calculate rates, branches, etc. <a href="https://www.oca.com.ar/Productos?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">OCA+1</a></p>



<hr>



<h3>2) What is the OCA sign-up form to use the plugin?</h3>



<p>To link your WordPress + WooCommerce with the Wanderlust plugin, you need:</p>



<ul>
<li><strong>OCA e-Pak / Express Pak client account</strong>, with:
<ul>
<li>Username (email you use to log into OCA e-Pak)</li>



<li>Password</li>



<li>OCA account number</li>



<li>CUIT and loaded contracts/operations (address, branch, etc.) <a href="https://shop.wanderlust-webdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Wanderlust-OCA-premium.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">shop.wanderlust-webdesign.com</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>



<p>The sign-up process works like this:</p>



<h4>🔹 Option A – Official OCA Express Pak form (recommended)</h4>



<ol>
<li>Go to the <strong>"OCA EXPRESS PAK – Account Opening Request"</strong> form.</li>



<li>There you fill in your company details, geographic area, website, and select <strong>"E-commerce Services / OCA Expresspak"</strong>. <a href="https://www1.oca.com.ar/ocaexpresspak/UsuarioWeB/apcuenta.asp?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">www1.oca.com.ar+1</a></li>
</ol>



<p>With that, OCA contacts you and creates your ecommerce client account.</p>



<blockquote class="is-layout-flow">
<p>If you want to find it quickly: search on Google<br><strong><code>"OCA EXPRESS PAK - Solicitud de Apertura de Cuenta"</code></strong><br>It's exactly that form.</p>
</blockquote>



<h4>🔹 Option B – Contact the executives recommended by the plugin itself</h4>



<p>On the official page for the <strong>"Wanderlust OCA for WooCommerce"</strong> plugin, they indicate that to open the account with OCA you can write to:</p>



<ul>
<li><strong>Lorena Marienberg – <a>lmarienberg@oca.com.ar</a></strong></li>



<li><strong>Martín Ibarrola – <a>mibarrola@oca.com.ar</a></strong></li>
</ul>



<p>They handle assisting you with opening the account to use the e-Pak API. <a href="https://es-ar.wordpress.org/plugins/wanderlust-oca-e-pak-shipping-rates/?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">WordPress.org Español (Argentina)</a></p>



<hr>



<h3>3) Once you have the account, what do you need to enter in the plugin?</h3>



<p>With your OCA account already active and the data they send you, in WooCommerce:</p>



<ol>
<li>Install and activate <strong>"Wanderlust OCA for WooCommerce"</strong> (or the Premium version). <a href="https://es-ar.wordpress.org/plugins/wanderlust-oca-e-pak-shipping-rates/?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">WordPress.org Español (Argentina)+1</a></li>



<li>In <strong>WooCommerce → Settings → Shipping → Shipping Zones</strong>, add the <strong>"OCA / Oca Express Pack"</strong> method. <a href="https://es-ar.wordpress.org/plugins/wanderlust-oca-e-pak-shipping-rates/?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">WordPress.org Español (Argentina)</a></li>



<li>In the method configuration, enter (according to the Wanderlust OCA Premium manual): <a href="https://shop.wanderlust-webdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Wanderlust-OCA-premium.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">shop.wanderlust-webdesign.com</a>
<ul>
<li>Account holder details: name, last name, email, address, postal code, city, province.</li>



<li><strong>OCA username</strong> (e-Pak email).</li>



<li><strong>OCA password</strong> (e-Pak password).</li>



<li><strong>OCA account number</strong> (found inside your account, under "Insurance conditions").</li>



<li><strong>CUIT</strong> in the format 00-00000000-0.</li>



<li>Operations/services (home delivery, branch pickup, etc.) assigned to you by OCA.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li>Make sure to set <strong>weight and dimensions</strong> on all products so it calculates rates. <a href="https://shop.wanderlust-webdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Wanderlust-OCA-premium.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">shop.wanderlust-webdesign.com+1</a></li>
</ol>
