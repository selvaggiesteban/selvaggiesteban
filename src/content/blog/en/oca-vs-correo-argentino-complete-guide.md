<p>If you have a WooCommerce store and are looking for a reliable shipping solution within Argentina, the most popular options are <strong>OCA</strong> and <strong>Correo Argentino</strong>. Both offer courier services with different features, advantages, and disadvantages. In this article, we help you decide which is the best option for your store, based on their features, costs, and ease of integration. 🚚</p>



<h2>What do you need to integrate OCA or Correo Argentino into WooCommerce? 🔧</h2>



<p>To integrate either of these courier services into your WooCommerce store, you need to use a <strong>specific plugin</strong> that handles communication between the WooCommerce system and the OCA or Correo Argentino platforms. 🛠️</p>



<p>The <strong>Wanderlust Codes</strong> plugin is a one-time payment option that allows you to manage shipments from these two carriers in your online store without the need for monthly subscriptions. 💳</p>



<h2>Services offered by OCA in WooCommerce 📬</h2>



<h3>Shipping types 🚚</h3>



<ul>
<li><strong>Standard home delivery:</strong> The package arrives directly at the recipient's door. 🏠</li>



<li><strong>Branch pickup:</strong> The recipient can pick up the package at the nearest OCA branch. 📍</li>



<li><strong>Express shipping:</strong> Fast service for urgent deliveries. ⚡</li>
</ul>



<h3>Additional features 🔍</h3>



<ul>
<li><strong>Real-time calculation:</strong> The plugin calculates shipping cost based on the package's weight, dimensions, and destination. 💰</li>



<li><strong>Label generation:</strong> Allows you to create shipping labels in PDF format, ready to print. 🖨️</li>



<li><strong>Shipment tracking:</strong> Customers can track their package status directly from your online store. 🔄</li>



<li><strong>Shipping insurance:</strong> You can add additional insurance for valuable shipments. 💎</li>
</ul>



<h3>OCA advantages ✅</h3>



<ul>
<li>Simple interface and easy to integrate with WooCommerce. 👨‍💻</li>



<li>Automation in label generation and order tracking. 🔄</li>



<li>Wide coverage across the entire country. 🇦🇷</li>
</ul>



<h3>OCA disadvantages ❌</h3>



<ul>
<li>Dependency on OCA's infrastructure for full functionality. 🔌</li>



<li>Service cost can be a factor if shipping volume is low. 💵</li>
</ul>



<h2>Services offered by Correo Argentino in WooCommerce 📬</h2>



<h3>Shipping types 🚚</h3>



<ul>
<li><strong>Home delivery:</strong> The package arrives directly to the recipient. 🏠</li>



<li><strong>Branch pickup:</strong> The recipient can pick up the package at a Correo Argentino branch. 📍</li>



<li><strong>Urgent shipping:</strong> Express service with faster delivery times. ⚡</li>



<li><strong>Standard shipping:</strong> Economical option for less urgent shipments. 💸</li>
</ul>



<h3>Additional features 🔍</h3>



<ul>
<li><strong>Real-time rate calculation:</strong> The plugin automatically calculates the shipping cost based on the package's characteristics and destination. 💰</li>



<li><strong>Label generation:</strong> Allows you to create shipping labels in PDF format. 🖨️</li>



<li><strong>Shipment tracking:</strong> Customers can track their package from the user dashboard. 🔄</li>



<li><strong>Shipping insurance:</strong> Allows you to add insurance to packages for additional protection. 💎</li>
</ul>



<h3>Correo Argentino advantages ✅</h3>



<ul>
<li>It is an official and recognized service in Argentina, which gives customers confidence. 🇦🇷</li>



<li>Both economical and fast options to adapt to different shipping needs. 💸⚡</li>



<li>The plugin is free and easy to integrate into WooCommerce. 🎉</li>
</ul>



<h3>Correo Argentino disadvantages ❌</h3>



<ul>
<li>Delivery times can be longer and less predictable in some regions. ⏳</li>



<li>May require more detailed manual configuration, especially for obtaining API credentials. 🔧</li>
</ul>



<h2>Do I need to register with OCA? 📝</h2>



<p>Yes, to use OCA's services, you need to register on their <a href="https://www6.oca.com.ar/plataformaenvios" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">OCA Shipping Platform</a>. This registration is free and will allow you to access the service's features, such as shipping quotes, label generation, and order tracking. 📑</p>



<p>The registration process is simple:</p>



<ol>
<li>Access the registration form on OCA's official website. 🌐</li>



<li>Complete the required information, such as your name, address, and tax details. 📝</li>



<li>Wait for validation by OCA, which may take up to 14 business days. ⏳</li>



<li>Once approved, you will receive your credentials to integrate OCA with your WooCommerce store. ✅</li>
</ol>
