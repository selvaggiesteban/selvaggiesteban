## Retain your customers with e-mail marketing

Of all existing digital channels, e-mail marketing still offers the highest return on investment in e-commerce. When a customer gives you their email, they open the door to intimate communication free of algorithms. I design strategic campaigns that educate, provide value, and convert simple subscribers into repeat buyers without saturating their inboxes.

## Automation in your e-mail marketing

The real magic happens when you set up automatic workflows. Advanced e-mail marketing allows your business to send the right message at the exact right time. If a customer adds a product to their cart but doesn't purchase, the system sends them an automatic reminder. I use robust tools that comply with privacy standards so your company can sell on autopilot.

### Segmentation and personalization

Mass emails are a thing of the past. I implement advanced segmentation based on behavior, purchase history, and engagement. Every subscriber receives content relevant to their interests, which dramatically increases open rates, click-through rates, and ultimately, conversions.
