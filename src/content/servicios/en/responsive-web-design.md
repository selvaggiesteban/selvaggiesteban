## The impact of good responsive web design

Nowadays, most users will browse your business through a mobile phone. Responsive web design is not a luxury, it's a technical obligation. I build digital ecosystems that fit like a glove to any resolution, from a smartphone to an ultrawide monitor, ensuring your message doesn't break and your customer doesn't get frustrated.

## Beyond aesthetics in responsive web design

A visually attractive site is useless if the code behind it is heavy or inefficient. My approach combines cutting-edge UI design with ultralight frontend development. I follow [Google's web development guidelines](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/crawling-indexing/mobile/mobile-sites-mobile-first-indexing) to ensure the underlying architecture is friendly to both human users and search engines.
